All Inclusive

Planning a wedding can be immensely stressful and intensely time-consuming – let us handle the work for you! The A to Z service will guide you through every step of the wedding planning process covering all creative, organisational, technical and logistical aspects of your wedding celebrations. The A to Z service is ideal for busy couples who need on the ground support to make their wedding happen We arrange an initial consultation, either face to face or via Skype, to discuss your needs and expectations in detail to design your wedding day together.

À La Carte

This package is for the Bride & Groom who need a little extra help tying up loose ends before their wedding day. Ideal if you have already started planning, partial wedding planning allows you to select which services you require help with and which you’ll handle on your own. In respect of your desires and budget, we will find a perfect fit for the things you’re missing. Examples include: finding the right venue, seeking and managing the caterer and musical entertainment for your reception, designing the wedding theme, and creating the decorations.
